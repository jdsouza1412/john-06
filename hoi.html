Migrating Winter Birds of India

India is home to several migratory birds during the winter season. Among them, the Indian Paradise Flycatcher, Bluethroat, and Northern Shoveler are notable visitors.

Indian Paradise Flycatcher

The Indian Paradise Flycatcher (Terpsiphone paradisi) is a stunning bird known for its long, elegant tail and contrasting black-and-white plumage. It migrates to southern parts of India during winter.

Bluethroat

The Bluethroat (Luscinia svecica) is a small passerine bird with a striking blue throat patch. It migrates from Europe and Central Asia to India in the winter, often seen in wetlands and grasslands.

Northern Shoveler

The Northern Shoveler (Anas clypeata) is a species of dabbling duck with a distinctive spatula-shaped bill. It migrates to India during winter and is commonly found in lakes and marshes.
